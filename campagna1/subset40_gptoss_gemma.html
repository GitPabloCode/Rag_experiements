Come si misura la distanza tra due BG consecutivi? Distance between balise groups is by definition the distance between closest balises of the two groups (i.e. between the Balise Reference Mark of the last one of the first group and the Balise Reference Mark of the first one of the second group). 12 La distanza tra due balise (BG) consecutive si misura dal marcatore di riferimento di una balise a quello dell’altra, cioè “from reference mark to reference mark”, e il valore massimo consentito è di 12 m [1]. ""| Rule | The maximum distance between two consecutive balises within the same group shall be 12 m from reference mark to reference mark.""
